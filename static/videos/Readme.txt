"Improving Robotic Imitation Learning via Trajectory Standardization"

1. OVERALL DESCRIPTION
-----------------------
This video accompanies the paper on Information-Standardized
Trajectory Resampling (ISR). The first part presents the ISR
method architecture and summarizes experimental results, including
a baseline comparison against time-uniform downsampling, an
ablation study on the acceleration weight, and a cross-operator
robustness analysis. The second part shows real-world inference
rollouts of pi_0.5 across three manipulation tasks: Place&Cover,
Place&Stack, and Push-T, performed by an AgileX Piper robot arm.
